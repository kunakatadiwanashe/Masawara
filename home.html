our values

Masawara was founded on a set of unwavering principles that have guided the way we invest in and mentor our portfolio companies. Sticking to our values - integrity, respect, humility, service & courage - has allowed us to build strategic relationships and ethically scale the businesses we support.

If it doesn’t feel right, we simply won’t do it. If it’s not about longterm impact that has the potential to uplift entire populations, it’s not for us

Our goal, above all else, is to make a positive contribution to Sub-Saharan Africa by creating financially strong companies that leave a lasting impact on the communities they serve.
